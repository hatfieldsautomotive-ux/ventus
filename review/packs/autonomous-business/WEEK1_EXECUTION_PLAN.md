## Kill Criteria
- <5 positive replies after 120 outbound
- 0 calls booked by day 4
- calls > 4 with zero close -> rewrite offer + risk reversal

## Scale Criteria
- 2+ closes in week 1
- 1 usable testimonial/case artifact
- repeatable outreach angle with >10% positive response
