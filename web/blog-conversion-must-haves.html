Website Conversion Must-Haves for New Businesses (Before You Spend on Ads)

A website should convert traffic into leads, not just look good. Before ad spend increases, make sure your pages are clear, trusted, and action-focused.

Must-have #1: Offer clarity above the fold

Say what you do, who you help, and what to do next immediately.

Must-have #2: Trust signals

Use testimonials, proof, and strong contact details. Trust reduces hesitation.

Must-have #3: One primary CTA

Too many choices lower action rates. Drive one primary outcome per page.

Must-have #4: Mobile-first experience

Fast load times, easy forms, and readable text are required for modern traffic.

Must-have #5: Structured intake and follow-up

Capture the right lead data, then respond fast with clear next steps.

Final takeaway

Build the conversion system first, then scale traffic into it. This protects ad spend and improves close rates.
