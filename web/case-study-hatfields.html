The Challenge

Competing With National Chains

Hatfield's Automotive had been serving the Chicago area for 15 years with exceptional service, but their outdated website was costing them customers.

  • ✗ No online booking — customers had to call during business hours
  • ✗ Not mobile-friendly — 65% of visitors bounced immediately
  • ✗ No pricing transparency — customers couldn't estimate costs
  • ✗ Poor Google ranking — invisible to new customers

JH

The Solution

A Complete Digital Transformation

Custom Online Booking System

We built a custom booking flow that lets customers schedule service appointments 24/7. The system integrates with Hatfield's existing calendar and sends automatic confirmations and reminders, reducing no-shows by 25%.

Service Cost Estimator

Transparency builds trust. We created an interactive estimator that lets customers get instant price ranges for common services. This feature alone increased qualified leads by 35%.

Local SEO Domination

We optimized every page for local search terms. Hatfield's now ranks #1 for "auto repair Chicago" and appears in the Google Local Pack — driving 60% more organic traffic.
